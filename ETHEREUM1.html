ETHEREUM



ABOUT THE STOCK:
Ethereum is a decentralized open-source blockchain platform that features its own cryptocurrency called Ether (ETH).
It was first described in a 2013 whitepaper by Vitalik Buterin and has since become one of the most prominent cryptocurrencies in the market.
Ethereum serves as a platform for numerous other cryptocurrencies and is also used for the execution of decentralized smart contracts.
