FIXED DEPOSITS - DCB BANKS



ABOUT THE STOCK:
DCB Bank, also known as Development Credit Bank, is a private sector bank in India.
DCB Bank offers fixed deposit (FD) accounts with various features and interest rates.

REASON FOR CHOOSING:
Investing in mutual funds is convenient and user-friendly.The risk associated with investing in the mutual funds is less when compared to shares.The mutual funds are also affordable .The interest rates offered by DCB investment funds are high. As of December 2023, the highest interest rate for regular investors is 6.95% for a tenure of 5-10 years. For senior citizens, the interest rate for the same tenure is 7.45%
